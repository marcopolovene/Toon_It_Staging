The Different Types of Photo-to-Cartoon Styles

Not all cartoon styles are the same. When you're choosing a tool or deciding what look you want, it helps to understand the main styles available:

3D Cartoon / Pixar Style

The most popular style for portraits. Think rounded features, expressive eyes, rich saturated colours, and that warm Pixar/Disney animation aesthetic. It works beautifully for people, pets, and family photos. The result looks like a character from an animated movie ‚Äî recognisable but elevated.

Anime / Studio Ghibli Style

Clean lines, large expressive eyes, detailed backgrounds, and the distinctly Japanese animation aesthetic that Ghibli perfected. Works wonderfully for portraits, landscapes, and scenes that have a contemplative or magical quality.

Watercolour Illustration

A softer, more painterly look. The photo becomes something that looks like a hand-painted watercolour illustration ‚Äî beautiful for pets, nature scenes, and portraits where you want an artistic rather than bold result.

Comic / Superhero Style

Bold outlines, dramatic colouring, and that classic comic book feel. Great for action shots, group photos, and anyone who wants to look like they're on the cover of a Marvel comic.

Storybook / Magical Style

Fairytale-inspired illustration. Soft lighting, magical atmosphere, and the feeling that the image belongs in a beautifully illustrated children's book. Perfect for family portraits and children's photos.
